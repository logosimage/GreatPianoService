Services

Tuning: With my combined experience as a piano technician, vocalist, musician and a fine ear for music for over 30 years, I will provide the highest quality tuning that you will receive from any piano technician. I combine these qualities with aural tuning and the use of a state of the art tuning device, which allow me to fine tune and diagnose any underlying problems with your piano. You will be pleased with my quality tunings and enjoy the beautiful sounds that will resonate from your piano once again.

Pitch Adjustment: A pitch adjustment becomes necessary when a piano has dropped too far (gone flat) in pitch or become too sharp in pitch. This generally happens when a piano has not been tuned for about two years or more or is subjected to extreme humidity conditions. When your piano has been tuned to its proper pitch you will benefit in following ways. A piano in proper pitch aids in ear training when taking music lessons. It makes the piano much more enjoyable to play. It allows you to play along with other instruments at the right pitch. I can bring your piano up to pitch and perform a fine-tuning in the same day.

Regulating: The piano action has over 900 mechanical wooden and felt parts that wear over time. Regulating is the tuning up of the mechanical functionality of the piano. This includes adjusting the feel and height of the keys, adjusting all of the action parts, conditioning or voicing the hammers, adjusting the pedals, etc. When your piano is regulated properly the feel, touch, speed and power are restored. The piano actually becomes much easier to play. Little hands and fingers taking lessons will find it much easier to play those more challenging pieces.

Repair and Restoration: From time to time and for different reason some pianos will be in need of repair or restoration. Repair services cover Key Top Replacement and key re-bushing and action rebuilding. Other major repairs such as sound board replacement, bridge repair and refinishing are contracted out to other piano repair specialist.

Purchase Consulting: You may be in the market for a new piano. Searching for a new piano can be a very time consuming and arduous task, especially if you're not sure where to begin. That's where I come in. I would be glad to provide you with free consultation over the phone as to where to begin and what to look for in a piano. When you find a piano you think fits your needs and it may be used from a private owner, I can come out and do a complete inspection on it for reasonable fee and advise as to its condition and purchase worthiness.

Humidity Control Systems: Your piano is made primarily of wood, a versatile and beautiful material ideal for piano construction. However, being made of wood, your piano is greatly affected by humidity. Seasonal and even daily changes in humidity cause wood parts to swell and shrink, affecting tuning stability and touch. Extreme swings in humidity can eventually cause wood to crack and glue joints to fail. A very practical and effective answer to humidity problems is to have a humidity control system installed in the piano itself. While not eliminating the need for regular piano maintenance, humidity control will allow more stable tunings by reducing the radical pitch changes your piano may experience through the seasons. I am a certified installer for the Damp-Chaser Humidity control system.

Service Contracts: The purpose of this Yearly Service Maintenance Agreements is simply to provide regular tuning cleaning and regulation services for your piano. I offer Yearly Service Maintenance Agreements generally for Churches, Schools and other institutions. Please call for details.

Cleaning: There are certain times and conditions when your piano will need different levels of cleaning. You may need me to give it a simple external dusting and vacuuming. Or it may need more extensive cleaning because of neglect or possibly smoke damage from a fire. In either case and at what ever level cleaning you need I'm here to help restore your piano to a satisfactory condition that you will be pleased with.
